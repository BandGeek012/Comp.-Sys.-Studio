The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxry yurts are permanent structures four feet off the gorund. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished wiht a queen-sized bed with down quilt nad gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax. Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weath with layers of clothing.
